The Improbable Story

Introduction

In the season of 2015-2016 two of the greatest records were broken. Golden State Warriors GSW set a regular season record of 73-9 which means they just lost 9 times in the whole season. Which is by far the most impressive accomplishment in NBA. With a record so good everyone had GSW as the NBA champion even before the playoffs started. What happened next was one of the rarest event in professional sports in all of North America. The Cleveland Cavaliers CLE were down in the NBA finals 3-1 to GSW, GSW just had to win one match to close the series and become champions. But GSW went down with the biggest upset in professional sports, GSW lost 3 straight games making CLE the champions.

The Top Tier

After the completion of the regular season, the top 4 teams with the highest chances of winning the championship was Cleveland Cavaliers CLE, Toronto Raptors TOR, Golden State Warriors GSW and San Antonio Spurs SAS. As usual the West is usually the stronger conference amongst East and West. We have GSW and SAS which had the highest chances of winning the championship and were from the West. Both the teams from the East had a very low chance of winning the championship.

The Best Against The Rest

When all was said and done the finals was between the GSW and CLE. In this bar graph we can see how the teams compared to their opponents in the regular season. The graph shows a clear point difference of +10 on average for GSW and +6 for CLE. Maintianing a +10 point difference in the west is really an achievement.

The Best Under Pressure

When we compare the performance of GSW and CLE in the finals to their regular season, we see that GSW completely choked and went from 114 points on average to 99 points per game, CLE also had a drop off but it isn't as drastic as that of GSW.

Who Dictated The Pace?

In this graph we can see that in reality CLE didn't play better than their regular season. In reality GSW really slowed down and their stats across the board dropped drastically. CLE pretty much played their game at their pace and brought GSW to their level.

Big money

Being the underdogs CLE had all odds against them. While all the predictions said GSW would win the championship the result differed and CLE won at last. Maybe a reference to The Chaos Theory. To give you a better understanding when GSW was up 3-1 in the series the chances of them winning was 94.1% if you would have bet 10,000 on CLE winning the series(before game 5), you would have made 120,000. No team in the professional sport has ever comeback from down 3-1, only CLE has done it, in many ways thanks to LeBron James.
